Who We Are NatureCare Rwanda is an environmental conservation initiative founded by young people passionate about nature protection and sustainable development. We are committed to conserving Rwanda’s natural resources through education, community participation, and practical conservation activities. Our Vision A Rwanda where nature is protected and future generations thrive in a healthy and sustainable environment. Our Mission To conserve Rwanda’s natural environment through environmental education, community action, youth engagement, and sustainable conservation practices. What We Do Promote environmental awareness in communities and schools Support tree planting and ecosystem restoration Organize community clean-up activities Encourage youth participation in environmental protection Advocate for sustainable environmental practices Our Values Environmental responsibility Community involvement Sustainability Integrity and teamwork
